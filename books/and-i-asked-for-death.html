AND I ASKED FOR DEATH

par Nathan Egwake Welo

en

Description

In this world where frustrations hide behind smiles, where weaknesses are concealed for fear of being judged, we live under the weight of a constant demand: to be strong, or at least to give the impression of being so, despite the suffering that gnaws at us. Through this autobiographical book, we have chosen to break the silence. This authentic testimony explores the causes and consequences of silence in the face of life's trials. It invites the reader to look at themselves from a different perspective, to break with the tendency to stifle their frustrations, and to expose them without fear in order to find freedom and healing in Christ. "And I asked for death" presents itself as a cry of revolt against the spirit of death and the suicidal thoughts that haunt many lives. This story is a call to confront pain, not to deny or mask it, but to lay it bare, face it fully, and entrust it to Jesus. He alone is able to heal broken hearts and lift up downcast souls.
